Katherine Maria Gorsuch


Dancer, mover, wanderlust adventurer, and a jill of all trades.

My hybrid expression of movement is derived from the many dance influences in my life. I received my formal dance training at Ballet Hispanico in New York City. I graduated from Hunter College with a BA in Anthropology and Linguistics--a degree that infused me with wanderlust, a craving to learn more languages, and the desire to engage with different cultures in the way that to me comes most naturally: through dance. My travels allowed me the opportunity to study flamenco in Madrid, tango in Buenos Aires, and aerial arts in Brazil.

A jill of all trades, when not dancing I am crafting, improvising, and finding art in whatever form, and forever learning. This website is an example of exactly that.

Enjoy!
